About Me

A little background on Andrew. Born in Columbus, GA, but raised in Barnesville, GA. Attended Lamar County High School. Played offense and defensive line for LCCHS, but baseball was my main sport. Tried out for Kansas City Royals baseball team in 1993, but did not make the squad.

Went on to attend Clayton College and State University where I received an Associates in Computer Science. I later on went on to attend Southern Polytechnic State University where I received my Bachelors in Computer Science.
